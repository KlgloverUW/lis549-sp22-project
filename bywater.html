By Water

Sailing

Explore Puget Sound and even duck out into the Pacific with wings on the water.

You do not have to know how to sail, or even someone with a sailboat, to get on saltwater in Seattle. Learn to sail from:

  • Sail Sand Point has introductory and intermediate series all summer. Learn more and sign up on their site.
  • The Mountaineers offers basic and advanced sailing as well as navigation classes. Learn more and sign up on their site.

Rowing

Get even closer to either fresh or salt water, on your own or in a team, by rowing. Whether you want to go out for just a morning or learn a lifelong sport here is where to connect to shells, sculls, and lessons:

  • University of Washington rents kayaks, canoes, and paddles by the hour to the UW community and public. Find more information on their site.
  • Lake Washington Rowing Club offers introductory classes to learn to row in a single boat or in a team. Once you have completed their course you are eligible to become a club member with boat borrowing privileges and access to their Lake Union dock. See how to sign up on their site.
