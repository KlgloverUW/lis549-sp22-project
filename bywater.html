By Water

Sailing

Explore Puget Sound and the Pacific with wings on the water.

You do not have to know how to sail to get saltwater experience in Seattle. Learn to sail from:

  • Sail Sand Point has introductory and intermediate series all summer. Learn more and sign up on their site.
  • The Mountaineers offer basic and advanced sailing as well as navigation classes. Learn more and sign up on their site.

Rowing

Get even closer to the water by rowing. Whether you want to go out for a morning or learn a lifelong sport here is where to connect to shells, sculls, and lessons:

  • University of Washington rents kayaks, canoes, and paddles by the hour to the UW community and public. Find more information on their site.
  • Lake Washington Rowing Club offers introductory classes to learn to row in a single boat or a team. Once you have completed their beginning course and a flip test you are eligible to become a club member with boat borrowing privileges and access to their Lake Union dock. See how to sign up on their site.
